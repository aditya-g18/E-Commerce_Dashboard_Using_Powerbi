## 📌 About This Project

An interactive **E-Commerce Sales Dashboard** built with **Microsoft Power BI**, 
analyzing sales performance across major Indian quick-commerce platforms.

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

| KPI | Value |
|-----|-------|
| 💰 Total Sales | ₹446.78M |
| 📦 Total Quantity | 8M |
| 🛒 Total Orders | 781K |
| 🚚 Avg Delivery Time | 16.50 |
| ⭐ Avg Customer Rating | 3.04 |

## 📈 Visualizations Included

- **Items by Company** – Flipkart, Blinkit, Swiggy Instamart, Zepto, Amazon Now, Dunzo, Jio Mart, Big Basket
- **Sales by City** – Gurgaon, Noida, Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru
- **Order Value by City (Top 3)** – Gurgaon | Noida | Delhi
- **Customer Rating by Company** – Blinkit leads with 348K ratings
- **Sales by Payment Method** – COD, Credit Card, Debit Card, UPI (nearly equal split ~25% each)
- **Sales by City (Bar Chart)** – Gurgaon tops with ₹45.24M

## 💡 Key Insights (from dashboard)

- Flipkart has the highest number of items sold (~980K)
- Blinkit has the highest customer ratings (~348K)
- Cash on Delivery generates the highest share of sales
- Gurgaon contributes the highest share of sales (21.98%)

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- Microsoft Power BI Desktop
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Power Query
- Slicers: City & Product Category filters
